Privacy Policy

MTR Next Train — Last updated: 2026-02-16

This Privacy Policy explains how MTR Next Train ("the App") handles information.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect personal data on our own servers. The App does not require account sign-in.

2. Location

The App may access your location to show nearby stations and train information. Location is used on-device for this feature.

  • Permission requested: While Using the App
  • You can disable location access anytime in iOS Settings

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple services and MTR public data APIs to provide functionality, including:

  • Apple MapKit / Core Location (nearby search and map display)
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit (subscriptions and purchase restore)
  • MTR API (next-train schedule data)

Data handling by these providers is governed by their own privacy terms.

4. In-App Purchases

Subscription processing is handled by Apple. We do not receive your full payment details.

5. Data Retention

App preferences (for example language, theme, pinned stations) are stored locally on your device.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

8. Contact

If you have questions, contact: mtrnexttrain26@gmail.com

私隱政策

MTR Next Train — 最後更新：2026-02-16

本私隱政策說明 MTR Next Train（「本 App」）如何處理資料。

1. 資料收集

我們不會在自家伺服器收集個人資料。本 App 無需帳戶登入。

2. 位置資料

本 App 可存取你的位置，以顯示附近車站及列車資訊。位置資料僅用於裝置內功能運作。

  • 要求權限：僅在使用 App 期間
  • 你可隨時在 iOS「設定」中關閉位置權限

3. 第三方服務

本 App 使用 Apple 服務及港鐵公開資料 API 提供功能，包括：

  • Apple MapKit / Core Location（附近搜尋與地圖顯示）
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit（訂閱與回復購買）
  • 港鐵 API（下一班車時間資料）

上述服務提供者的資料處理方式受其私隱條款約束。

4. App 內購買

訂閱付款由 Apple 處理；我們不會取得你的完整付款資料。

5. 資料保存

App 偏好設定（例如語言、主題、釘選車站）會儲存在你的裝置本機。

6. 兒童私隱

本 App 並非以 13 歲以下兒童為對象，亦不會故意收集兒童個人資料。

7. 政策更新

本政策可能不時更新，更新版本會於本頁公布並標示最新日期。

8. 聯絡我們

如有查詢，請電郵至：support@example.com
